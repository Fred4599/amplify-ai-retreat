Prepare

Come with your ask.

Wednesday morning starts with introductions. If you don't know the business problem you came to solve, the room can't help you move it.

“What's the one bottleneck in my business that, if AI helped me solve it this week, would change everything?”

Your Ask

Make it specific.

Too vague

“I want to use AI more.”

Sounds familiar — and it won't get you a win by Wednesday night.

Ready for the room

“I need a way to follow up leads within an hour without hiring.”

Clear problem. Clear win. Something we can build toward.

Write it down before Wednesday. Sit with it at breakfast if you need to — but don't walk into introductions empty-handed.
